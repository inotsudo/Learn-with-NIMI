# Level 2 — Preschool Curriculum Blueprint

**Age Range:** 3 – 5 years
**Framework:** Language Expansion & Creative Discovery
**Total Units:** 52 | **Blocks:** 13 × 4 units
**Languages:** English · Français · Kinyarwanda

## Guiding Principles
- Language-rich activities in all three languages; sentence-level expression expected
- Early literacy embedded: rhyme, alliteration, story structure, descriptive vocabulary
- Early numeracy embedded: counting to 20, sorting by two attributes, simple patterns
- Inquiry-first: predict before exploring, describe after, connect to prior knowledge
- Rwanda's history, geography, culture, and community as the contextual backbone

## Complexity Profile
| Category | Preschool Approach |
|---|---|
| 🎵 Morning Song | Multilingual song with vocabulary, rhyme, or counting woven in |
| 🏃 Movement Mission | Structured activity with partner work, directional language, rule-following |
| 🎨 Mission Artistique | Process art with colour mixing, intentional mark-making, and description |
| 🏛️ Mission Historique | Story with discussion, character identification, and prediction questions |
| 🔍 Mission Zoom | Observe, describe, compare, and ask questions about what is seen |
| 🌱 Mission Discovery | Sort by two attributes, predict outcomes, classify, and experiment |
| 📖 FlipFlop Book | Read with support; identify character, setting, and problem; retell key events |
| 🖍️ Coloring Book | Colour with attention to pattern, detail, and deliberate colour choice |

## Block 1 — Identity & Welcome (Units 1–4)

**Development Focus:** Expressive greetings, social language, and trilingual introduction

| 🏃 Movement Mission | Perform a greeting-dance sequence: shake hands, wave, bow, and spin with a partner |
