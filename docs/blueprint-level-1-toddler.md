# Level 1 — Toddler Curriculum Blueprint

**Age Range:** 18 months – 3 years
**Framework:** Sensory Exploration & First Words
**Total Units:** 52 | **Blocks:** 13 × 4 units
**Languages:** English · Français · Kinyarwanda

## Guiding Principles
- Single-concept per activity; high repetition
- Sensory-first learning (touch, taste, smell, hear, see)
- Adult-guided with maximum scaffolding
- Parallel vocabulary in EN / FR / RW woven into every category
- Rwanda's landscapes, animals, foods, and traditions as the primary cultural lens

## Block 1 — Identity & Welcome (Units 1–4)

### Unit 1 — Hello, World!
**Development Focus:** First greetings, self-awareness, and beginning social interaction
**Learning Outcomes:**
1. Respond to their own name when called
2. Imitate a simple hello gesture (wave, smile)
3. Hear and recognise basic greetings in English, French, and Kinyarwanda

| Category | Objective |
|---|---|
| 🎵 Morning Song | Sing a call-and-response "Hello" song using name and wave gesture |
| 🏃 Movement Mission | Waddle, wave, and walk toward a caregiver on cue in a "Come to me!" movement game |
| 🎨 Mission Artistique | Make a hand-print greeting card using a single bold colour |
| 🏛️ Mission Historique | Listen to a brief story about a baby's first morning and first greeting with family |
| 🔍 Mission Zoom | Watch smiling faces from Rwanda saying "Muraho", "Bonjour", "Hello" in sequence |
| 🌱 Mission Discovery | Touch a mirror and recognise their own reflection; point to their face |
| 📖 FlipFlop Book | Flip through a "Hello, Friend!" picture book showing children greeting each other |
| 🖍️ Coloring Book | Colour a large, simple smiling face template |

### Unit 2 — My Name Is…
**Development Focus:** Name recognition, self-identity, and first label vocabulary
**Learning Outcomes:**
1. Point to themselves when their name is said
2. Attempt to say or sign their own name
3. Recognise their photo or reflection as "me"

| Category | Objective |
|---|---|
| 🎵 Morning Song | Sing a personalised name song where each child's name is sung in the chorus |
| 🏃 Movement Mission | Play "Run to your name card" — toddlers move to their photo on the mat |
| 🎨 Mission Artistique | Trace or stamp their first initial in bold paint on paper |
| 🏛️ Mission Historique | Listen to the story of how a Rwandan child receives their name at the naming ceremony |
| 🔍 Mission Zoom | Watch a short video of children from Rwanda introducing themselves by name |
| 🌱 Mission Discovery | Sort name-card photos by matching toddlers' own photo to their mirror reflection |
| 📖 FlipFlop Book | Read "What Is Your Name?" — a book of diverse children saying their names |
| 🖍️ Coloring Book | Colour a name-badge template with their initial letter at the centre |

### Unit 3 — My Five Senses
**Development Focus:** Sensory vocabulary and sensory self-regulation
**Learning Outcomes:**
1. Point to ears, eyes, nose, mouth, and hands on cue
2. Imitate sensory actions (sniff, taste, listen, look, touch)
3. Use one-word labels for basic sensory experiences (hot, soft, loud)

| Category | Objective |
|---|---|
| 🎵 Morning Song | Sing "I Can See, I Can Hear" — a song naming each sense with a matching gesture |
| 🏃 Movement Mission | Play a freeze-and-sense game: freeze to listen, unfreeze to touch, tiptoe to sniff |

| 🌱 Mission Discovery | Explore a sensory tray with sand, water, leaves, and smooth pebbles |
| 📖 FlipFlop Book | Read a tactile "touch-and-feel" story: "What Does This Feel Like?" |

1. Participate in a community celebration activity
2. Express gratitude for their learning journey
3. Name one thing they have learned and one thing they look forward to

| Category | Objective |
|---|---|
| 🎵 Morning Song | Sing "We Did It Together!" — a celebratory graduation-style song |
| 🏃 Movement Mission | Perform a joyful community dance: circle, clap, and celebrate together |
| 🎨 Mission Artistique | Create a self-portrait showing how much they have grown this year |
| 🏛️ Mission Historique | Listen to a story about the Umuganura harvest festival — celebrating abundance |
| 🔍 Mission Zoom | Watch Rwandan children celebrating Umuganura and community events |
| 🌱 Mission Discovery | Review favourite discoveries from the year: what was the most interesting? |
| 📖 FlipFlop Book | Read "The Big Celebration" — a story of a whole village gathering to celebrate |
| 🖍️ Coloring Book | Colour a celebration scene: dancing, drums, food, smiling faces, and community |
