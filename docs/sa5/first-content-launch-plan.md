## 7. Publishing Schedule

### Recommended: Staged Release

| Week | Activity | Stories Live |
|---|---|---|
| Week -2 | Internal testing, content review | 0 |
| Week -1 | Bug fixes, final content upload, QA | 0 |
| **Week 0** | **Soft launch — invite 20 families** | **4** |
| Week 1 | Monitor, fix issues, gather feedback | 4 |
| Week 2 | Public launch announcement | 4 |
| Week 3 | First new story planning begins | 4 |
| Week 4 | Review analytics, plan Story 5 | 4 |

### Why Staged Release (Not Staggered)

**Staggered (1 story per week):** Children who finish Story 1 in Week 1 hit a wall — nothing to do until Week 2. Frustrating for engaged families.

**All 4 at once:** Children always have the next story available. The unlock progression (complete Story 1 → Story 2 unlocks) naturally gates content. No artificial waiting.

**Recommendation: Launch all 4 stories simultaneously** with a soft launch to 20 pilot families for Week 0 testing.

## Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Illustration delays | Medium | High — blocks FlipFlop + Coloring | Start illustrations first, they're the longest lead |
| Translation delays | Medium | Medium — EN can launch without FR/RW | Launch EN-first, add languages in Week 1-2 |
| Audio recording scheduling | High | Medium — voice talent availability | Book sessions 2 weeks in advance |
| Video production | High | Medium — most complex asset | Accept "Coming Soon" for bonus video at launch if needed |
| Song licensing | Low | High — if using existing music | Write original songs (simple children's melodies) |
| Content review bottleneck | Medium | Low — delays publishing | Parallel review: different reviewers per story |

### Acceptable Launch Gaps

If timeline is tight, these assets can be added post-launch:

| Asset | Impact of Missing | Add By |
|---|---|---|
| Bonus Video | "Coming Soon" fallback shown | Week +2 |
| FR/RW narration | EN narration works, TTS fallback | Week +2 |
| Intro Video | Story works without intro | Week +1 |
| Theme Song | Not critical for story flow | Week +1 |
| Meet Characters video | Not critical | Week +2 |

Mission content (FlipFlop, PDF, Coloring, Move, Sing) should NOT launch with gaps — these are the core educational experience.

## Launch Recommendation

### Recommended: Soft Launch

**Strategy:** Launch all 4 stories to 20 invited pilot families. English complete, French/Kinyarwanda in progress. Monitor for 1 week, fix issues, then announce publicly.

**Justification:**
1. Real children testing reveals issues mockups cannot
2. 20 families is enough for feedback, small enough to support individually
3. Pilot families become advocates for public launch
4. French/Kinyarwanda can ship in Week 1-2 without blocking EN launch
5. Risk is contained — any critical bug affects 20 families, not 200
